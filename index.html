About Us

We are a health-tech startup focused on elderly care and wellness. Our flagship product, the Vital Band, is a wearable health monitoring device that tracks 25+ health and fitness metrics including SpO2, heart rate, falls, motion, and GPS location. Our goal is to empower families with real-time health insights and timely alerts.
